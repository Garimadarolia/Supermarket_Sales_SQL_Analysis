# Supermarket_Sales_SQL_Analysis

## Objective
Analyze retail sales data to identify revenue patterns, customer behavior, and product performance.

## Dataset
Supermarket Sales Dataset

## Tools Used
- MySQL
- SQL (Aggregation, GROUP BY, Subqueries, Filtering, Sorting)

## Analysis Performed
- Revenue analysis
- Product performance
- Customer segmentation
- Payment trends

## Key Insights
1. The highest revenue is generated by Naypyitaw.
2. Food and beverages contributes the most i.e., 17.38 % to total sales.
3. Most customer prefer E-Wallet.
4. Members generates more revenue with an average spending of 333.74.
5. High-value transactions contribute significantly to total sales.
6. Giza branch performed best by generating a revenue of 110568.86 and with an average rating of 7.02.
7. Electronic accessories is the most popular product line with 971 units sold.
